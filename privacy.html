Overview

Ranger Ops respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and how to contact us.

Information We Collect

The app is intended to be playable without requiring a user account. Game progress, scores, coins, equipped weapons, and upgrade status may be stored locally on your device.

Personal Information

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address through the app unless you choose to contact us directly.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Future versions may use basic analytics or crash diagnostics to help improve performance and fix bugs. Any analytics should be limited to app usage and technical performance data.

Children's Privacy

Ranger Ops is designed as a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so it can be removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may only be shared when required by law, to protect the app, or to operate necessary services such as app hosting, analytics, or crash reporting.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
