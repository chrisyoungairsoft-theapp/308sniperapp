About the app

Arcade missions, weapon upgrades, and vehicle challenges.

Ranger Ops is designed for players who enjoy simple controls, fast missions, and clear progression. Pick a ranger, choose a mission, collect coins, unlock weapons, upgrade tanks, and compete for better scores.

Mission Selection

Choose from Run & Shoot, Target Practice, and Vehicle Mode. Each mode offers a different way to play and improve your score.

Weapons Room

Save coins and equip weapons such as a knife, pistol, dual pistols, and SMG as you progress through the game.

Workshop

Use workshop coins to unlock tanks and upgrade engine, fuel, and cannon stats for vehicle missions.
